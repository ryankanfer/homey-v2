## Design Context

### Users
Mixed NYC real estate audience: first-time buyers overwhelmed by the market, savvy repeat buyers seeking edge, and high-net-worth buyers ($2M+) expecting white-glove service. Also agents using the platform to manage clients. All share one thing: high stakes, high anxiety, high information need. They arrive stressed and leave (ideally) feeling like they have a trusted advisor in their corner.

### Brand Personality
**Refined. Elevated. Quiet.**

homey. is the private advisor you didn't know you could afford. It doesn't sell — it reveals. It doesn't shout — it observes. The brand speaks softly and with precision. Every word earns its place. Every interaction feels intentional.

Three-word emotional target: **Seen. Understood. Ready.**

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference**: Loewe, Bottega Veneta — luxury restraint, generous negative space, editorial typography, nothing decorative that doesn't earn its presence
- **Anti-reference**: Zillow/StreetEasy (utilitarian grid, commodity feel), Generic SaaS dashboard (sidebar nav, metric tiles, card soup)
- **Theme**: Dark only. Near-black `#0D0D0B` background. Warm sand gold `#C8B89A` as the single accent — not cold blue, not neon, not gradient
- **Typography**: Playfair Display serif for display/questions (italic for intimate moments), Inter for body/labels. Uppercase tracking-widest micro-labels feel like editorial bylines, not UI chrome
- **Corners**: Sharp. No rounded cards. No pill buttons unless contextually intentional
- **Motion**: Framer Motion, ease-out-expo, staggered reveals. Never bounce. Never elastic. Transitions that feel like turning a page, not a loading spinner

### Onboarding Emotional Arc
1. **Step 1–2**: Intrigue — "What is this building for me?"
2. **Step 3–4**: Recognition — "This knows my situation"
3. **Step 5–6**: Anticipation — the profile is crystallizing
4. **Synthesis**: Arrival — "I have an advisor now"

Each question should feel like a smart person asking it in person — not a form field with a label. The interface recedes; the question leads.

### Design Principles
1. **The advisor recedes** — UI chrome disappears, the question/content is the hero
2. **Restraint is confidence** — more space, fewer elements, nothing decorative without purpose
3. **Serif for intimacy** — italic Playfair Display signals a human conversation, not a data entry task
4. **Sand gold, sparingly** — the accent carries trust and warmth; overuse dilutes it
5. **Every empty state teaches** — nothing is blank, everything points forward
6. **Progressive revelation** — the profile builds visibly; users should feel it taking shape
